Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why does the app ask for Calendar permission?
    PenTimer saves your focus sessions as events in Apple Calendar (start and end times).
  • Does PenTimer collect my data?
    No. The app runs locally and does not collect or sell user data.
  • How do I enable Calendar access?
    Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Calendars and allow PenTimer.

Troubleshooting

  • Calendar access not granted
    Enable it in Settings → Privacy & Security → Calendars.
  • Events not showing
    Check the correct calendar is enabled in the Calendar app, then restart PenTimer.

Privacy

PenTimer can save sessions to Apple Calendar only with your permission. The app does not require accounts and does not track users.
