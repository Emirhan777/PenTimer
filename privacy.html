Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

PenTimer is designed to respect your privacy. This app does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to any external servers.

Data Collection

PenTimer does not collect any user data. The app does not use analytics, tracking technologies, or advertising frameworks.

Calendar Access

PenTimer can save focus sessions as events in Apple Calendar. Calendar access is optional and requires your explicit permission.

Calendar data is handled entirely on your device using Apple’s EventKit framework. PenTimer does not read, store, or transmit calendar data outside your device.

Data Storage

All app functionality runs locally on your device. No user data is uploaded, shared, or backed up to external services.

Third-Party Services

PenTimer does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children’s Privacy

PenTimer does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be made available on this page.
